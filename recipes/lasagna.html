Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna Dish

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and drain the excess fat.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the lasagna noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
