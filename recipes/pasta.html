Pasta Recipe

Pasta Dish

Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the noodles and return them to the pot.
  3. Add the tomato sauce and stir to combine.
  4. Serve the pasta with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
