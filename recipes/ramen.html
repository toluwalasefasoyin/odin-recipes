Ramen Recipe

Ramen Dish

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Chicken broth
  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Bring the chicken broth to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add the ramen noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Transfer the noodles to a bowl and pour the hot broth over them.
  4. Top with a soft-boiled egg and green onions.
